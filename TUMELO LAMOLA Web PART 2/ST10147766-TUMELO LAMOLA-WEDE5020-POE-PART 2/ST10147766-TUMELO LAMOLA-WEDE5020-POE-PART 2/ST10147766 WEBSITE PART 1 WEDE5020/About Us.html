OPERATING PLAN

3D-PRINTING TECH.SUPP offer small and big organizations different plans to sustain them. We offer small companies a contract of 6 months with limited credit to pay back for the products they purchased from us like our electronic assembly products. We offer large organizations a long duration of time so that they could be able to come and do their second purchase. We offer them a long duration of time to pay all the amount because they usually do large purchases, and we are their supplier. Our customers that require IT services need to pay the full amount. We offer different services, so our clients will need to contact our offices for full quotation.
